About Us

Apaar Solutions is a reputable provider of comprehensive security and communication systems, offering a wide range of products and services designed to meet diverse security needs. Here are several reasons for choosing Apaar Solutions:

  • Extensive Product Portfolio:

    • Video Surveillance Systems: CCTV Cameras wired and wireless.

    • Home Security Solutions: Video door phones, intrusion alarm systems, fire alarm systems and gate automation.

    • Smart Office Solutions: Fingerprint & facial recognition time attendance machines, door access control systems, EPABX systems, projectors, display solutions and PA systems.

  • Proven Track Record: Established in 2011, Apaar Solutions has over a decade of experience in delivering high-quality security solutions. Our long-standing presence in the industry underscores our reliability and commitment to excellence.

  • Customer Satisfaction: Clients have expressed high levels of satisfaction with Apaar Solutions services. Reviews highlight the professionalism, efficiency, and timely delivery provided by our team.

  • Local Presence: With a physical office located at SCO 108, Sector 47-C, Chandigarh, Apaar Solutions offers accessible customer support and services. Our local presence ensures prompt assistance and personalized service for clients in the region.

  • Commitment to Quality: Apaar Solutions is dedicated to providing high-quality products and services. Our focus on quality ensures that clients receive reliable and effective security solutions tailored to their specific needs.
