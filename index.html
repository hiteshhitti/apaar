Our Products

CCTV Surveillance

Protect your home, office, or commercial premises with our state-of-the-art CCTV surveillance systems. Whether you want to monitor activity in real-time or need playback for incidents, our solutions provide reliable, high-definition footage day and night. Perfect for residential colonies, retail stores, warehouses, and schools.

  • Full HD/4K video clarity with night vision
  • Mobile access for live view and playback
  • Motion detection & instant alerts
  • Supports cloud and local recording

Video Door Phone (VDP)

Stay in control of who enters your premises with our advanced Video Door Phone systems. These devices let you see and talk to visitors before opening the door, adding an essential layer of security for families and professionals alike.

  • Clear two-way audio & video communication
  • Night vision camera for low-light monitoring
  • Remote door unlocking (optional models)
  • Multiple indoor screens for multi-room control

Security Alarm Systems

Prevent thefts and break-ins before they happen. Our alarm systems are equipped with intelligent sensors that detect suspicious activity and trigger loud sirens and mobile notifications. Ideal for jewelry shops, offices, godowns, and villas.

  • Instant alerts for door/window breach or motion
  • Wireless & wired sensor options available
  • Connects with CCTV and VDP systems
  • Battery backup and remote control

Walkie Talkie

Maintain instant and uninterrupted communication without depending on mobile networks. Our walkie talkies are rugged, long-range, and reliable — making them the perfect solution for events, security teams, hotels, construction sites, and industrial zones.

  • Crystal-clear voice transmission over long range
  • Rechargeable batteries with long standby time
  • License-free models available
  • Compact, durable & weather-resistant build

EPABX Systems

Organize communication within your business effortlessly. EPABX (Electronic Private Automatic Branch Exchange) allows you to manage internal calls, route external calls efficiently, and enhance professional image — a must-have for any office or institution.

  • Connects multiple internal extensions seamlessly
  • Call transfer, hold, voicemail, and intercom features
  • Expandable from 4 to 100+ lines
  • Supports analog, digital, and IP-based systems
